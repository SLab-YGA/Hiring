MTS (Member of Technical Staff), Quantum Processor Materials 

Job Summary: 
Innovation and research on materials have long been an important cornerstone for quantum technology advancement. 
The understanding and manipulation of the interplay between quantum-state coherence and material properties are the key enablers for future practical quantum processor. 
We strive for top-quality and efficient material research and fabrication through innovation and close collaboration with both industrial and academic partners. 
We are seeking creative and results-driven researchers and engineers to join our dynamic, multidisciplinary team. 
All levels of experience are welcome. 
Candidates with proven leadership skills may be considered for a Team Lead position.

Key Responsibilities:
• Based on your background and expertise, you will be responsible for one or more modules in the research of material- and fabrication-related projects. Your responsibilities may include, but are not limited to:
  ￮ Research on superconducting/dielectric/thin film materials for processor performance improvement 
  ￮ Basic/advanced material characterization (Diffraction/Topography/Transport/Spectrometry/etc.)
  ￮ Fabrication process modules (e.g., lithography, deposition, etching, thinning/polishing)
  ￮ Cryogenic physical property testing and microwave testing
• Work closely with academic and industrial partners
• Lead research projects and deliver timely impactful results

Qualifications: 
• An advanced degree or a proven track record of strong performance in at least one of these fields: experimental condensed matter physics, materials science, cryogenic measurement, superconducting electronics fabrication, semiconductor fabrication, optoelectronics, MEMS
• Passion and endurance for quantum computing and long-term research projects
• Proficiency in programming languages such as Phython, C/C++ are preferred
